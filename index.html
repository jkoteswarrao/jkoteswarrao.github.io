Bio:

I am currently serving as a faculty member at Graphic Era University. I did my PhD under the supervision of A/Prof Cai Jianfei and A/Prof Yuan Junsong at Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. I am primarily interested in computer vision and multimedia research. I did my B.Tech in Electrical Engineering at Indian Institute of Technology Roorkee (IIT R). I have also worked with ADSC and Lenskart.
